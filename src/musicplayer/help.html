Music Player

Music Player Application has the follow functions:

Add Songs - To add a song you need to click on the AddSongs button and select the songs(MP3 files).The first song will start playing.

Search - To search for a song you need to enter the song title and click on the Search button. If the Song is found, it will start playing.

Save - To save the tracklist on a CSV file you need to click on the Save button.

Play - If you stop or pause a song you can play it, you just need to click on the Play button.

Stop - To stop the current song, you need to click on the Stop button.

Pause - To Pause the current song, you need to click on the Pause button.

First - To play the first song, you need to click on the First button.

Previous - To play the previous song, you need to click on the Previous button.

Next - To play the next song, you need to click on the Next button.

Last - To play the last song, you need to click on the Last button.
